Research Interests

Exploring the intersection of computational mechanics, material science, and advanced simulation techniques.

Finite Element Analysis

Developing high-fidelity computational models to simulate complex physical phenomena, with a focus on explicit dynamics and non-linear material behavior.

Composite Materials

Investigating the micromechanics of advanced composites like UHMWPE. My work aims to predict failure modes under extreme loading conditions.

Impact Dynamics

Studying high-velocity impact events. I use LS-DYNA and custom C++ codes to model ballistic impact and shock wave propagation in solids.
